Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for admission?

You can apply online through our website or visit any of our campuses with the required documents. Contact our admissions office for the complete list of requirements.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer merit-based and need-based scholarships. Please contact our admissions office to learn more about available opportunities.

What is your curriculum?

We offer both Nigerian and British curricula, preparing students for IGCSE, Checkpoint, and Cambridge A & O Levels.

Are boarding facilities available?

Yes, we have boarding facilities at select campuses. Please contact us to confirm availability and booking details.

What extracurricular activities are offered?

We offer sports, music, arts, debate, coding, entrepreneurship programs, and many other activities to develop well-rounded students.

When can I visit the school?

We welcome campus visits! Please schedule an appointment with our admissions office at least 2 days in advance.
