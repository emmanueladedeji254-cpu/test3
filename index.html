About Honeyland Schools

Building Leaders, Inspiring Minds, Shaping Futures

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive, world-class education that develops critical thinking, creativity, and character in every student. We cultivate leaders who will make positive impacts on their communities and the world.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Nigeria, recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and holistic development. Creating a generation of globally competitive, ethical, and compassionate leaders.

Our Values

Excellence, Integrity, Inclusivity, Creativity, Respect, and Responsibility. These values guide everything we do and shape the character of our students.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a leading educational institution

2010

Foundation Year

Honeyland Schools was founded with a vision to revolutionize education in Nigeria. We started with a small group of dedicated educators and a commitment to excellence.

2012

First Expansion

Opened our second campus, expanding our reach and bringing quality education to more families across Lagos. Our enrollment grew significantly.

2015

Academic Excellence Recognition

Our students achieved outstanding results in international exams (IGCSE, Cambridge). Started offering dual curriculum combining Nigerian and British education standards.

2017

Montessori Integration

Introduced the renowned Montessori method in our preschool and primary levels, focusing on child-centered learning and development of critical thinking skills.

2019

Facilities Upgrade

Invested in state-of-the-art facilities including modern laboratories, computer labs, sports complexes, and boarding facilities to enhance learning experience.

2021

Multi-Campus Growth

Established 7 thriving campuses across Lagos (Ikeja, Lekki, Magodo, Baruwa, Ipaja, Isolo, Ajasa) serving thousands of students from diverse backgrounds.

2023

Innovation & Digital Learning

Implemented advanced digital learning platforms and introduced coding, robotics, and entrepreneurship programs to prepare students for the 21st century.

2025

Global Excellence

Celebrated numerous awards and accolades in academics, sports, and arts. Students gaining admissions to top universities worldwide, realizing our vision of global competitiveness.
